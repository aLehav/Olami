Shannon Scovel is the Editor-in-Chief for The Eagle and a junior journalism student from Cary, North Carolina. She has a passion for sports and has contributed articles to the Raleigh News and Observer and Cary News. Shannon also works as a collegiate correspondent for AOL Sports and interned with USA Today College this summer. 

When she is not writing about the American University Eagles, Shannon is competing as a member on the AU varsity swim team, racing triathlons for Team USA or reading inspirational sports books. She also serves as a student ambassador for AU’s School of Communication where she works with peers and faculty to promote the communication program to current and prospective students.
