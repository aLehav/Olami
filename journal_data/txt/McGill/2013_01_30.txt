Introducing “The Basics,” The McGill Daily’s latest radio series.
In each short episode, we’ll provide you with a condensed summary of a long-term issue that we’re reporting on in the news section, giving you the background you need to start following the story. Consider them your news cheat-sheets. In our first episode, Multimedia Editor Kate McGillivray breaks down the issue of asbestos research at McGill. Enjoy!
If you know a topic you’d like to see an episode of “The Basics” address, e-mail radio@mcgilldaily.com.
