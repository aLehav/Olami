Some have called us the generation that grew up with Harry Potter. Just as Harry, Ron, and Hermione advanced from year to year, we matured along with them. But in half an hour, "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2"—the final installment of the movie series based on J.K. Rowling's books—will be released in the United States. Then what happens?
Earlier today, lucky Harvard affiliates got to attend a special screening of the movie. We asked a few people waiting in line what the condclusion of Harry's story meant to them. Is this the end of our childhood?
