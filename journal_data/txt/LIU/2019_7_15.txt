Caroline Ryan (’18), is currently working full-time for
Anton Media Group in Mineola. She is the Editor of two newspapers, The
Roslyn News and The Glen Cove Record Pilot. 

Caroline was The Pioneer’s
 Editor-In-Chief from
2017-18 during her junior and senior year. She was also the Institute 
Director for the Summer Journalism Institute for High School Students 
during 2018. Caroline contributes her success now to
The Pioneer and from having learned so much from her professors at Post.
Caroline encourages students interested in journalism to get involved as
 soon
as possible and to stay passionate
