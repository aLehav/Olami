On the front page of the April 9 issue, the Pioneer reported about senior Health Science major Kristin McDonough, who was auditioning to join the New York Jets Flight Crew (the Jets’ dance and cheer team). The Pioneer is thrilled to report that on April 10, McDonough was selected to be a member of the Flight Crew!
