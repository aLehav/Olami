Melanie Spina ’16, the Pioneer’s 2016 Opinions Editor and 2015 Assistant Arts & Entertainment Editor, is working for Huge, a creative digital agency in Brooklyn. As a part of the content strategy team, Melanie started off on a project for ExxonMobil’s career and family website and then worked on a project for Google’s platform “Think with Google.” She wrote, “It’s really been quite the experience. What was supposed to be only a one month job has now turned to six months and I am absolutely loving it and learning a lot.”
