“I’m shocked.”
That was the reaction of many UCF students following Tuesday night’s election.
In the early hours of Wednesday morning it was announced that Donald J. Trump would be elected the 45th President of the United States.
Millions were in awe.
After nearly two years of campaigning in an election that has polarized the nation, some Americans are having a hard time coping with the results.
Knight News interviewed a number of UCF students in the Student Union asking what they thought about the election results, and whether or not they believed that the United States could unify behind President Trump.
