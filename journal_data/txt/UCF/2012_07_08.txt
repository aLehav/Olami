Central Florida Author, Ken Pelham, talks about his new book “Brigands Key” being released July 18th.
Pelham, an alumus of the University of Florida, is a third generation Floridian with a deep knowledge for the archeology and history of the state.
This all plays into the novel, which is a suspense thriller that features a Category 5 hurricane, lethal plagues, and murder.
Sound interesting? Watch the interview Knightnews.com did with him below.
