Orange County deputies are investigating after a woman’s body was found in a car in the Wal-Mart parking lot on East Colonial Drive early Saturday morning, WFTV is reporting.
According to WFTV, deputies and firefighters found the woman’s body after responding to a hazmat situation in the parking lot around 7:15 on Saturday morning.
Deputies told WFTV that chemicals were found in the car and it could be a possible suicide.
