Free to University of Central Florida students and alumni, tailgating reservations for the UCF Spring football game opened for claims.
The Knights on the Mall online reservation system can be accessed to select a spot for the first game that will be led under the program’s new head coach – Scott Frost.
The Spring game will take place on April 16 at 7 p.m., the first to be “preceded by a motocross show and concerts,” according to university officials.
Memory Mall opens at noon and parking can be found in Garage-C, Garage-D, Garage-H, and Lot C2. At 9 a.m., lots E3, E6, softball as well as the baseball fields for the general public. Permit parking will be available in lots E1, E2, and E8.
