Recent UCF grad Megan Clementi was crowned Miss Florida USA Saturday during the competition on Hollywood Beach.
In addition to being a proud Knight with a degree in Electrical Engineering, Clementi is a dancer for the Orlando Magic. She also hosts “UKnighted” on UCFTV Wednesday’s at noon.
The Jupiter, Fla. native beat out 51 regional pageant winners from across the state.
She will now go on to compete in the Miss USA Pageant. If she wins there, she’ll move on to compete for Miss Universe.
UCF is no stranger to beauty queens. UCF’s own Ericka Dunlap was crowned Miss America recently.
The pageant will re-air Sunday, July 26 at 4 p.m. on WRDQ, Channel 27, Brighthouse channel 10.
