It’s safe to say that Chipotle is one of the more popular food chains visited by our generation, and now it’s going to be even more popular. 
All chipotle locations will be offering students a free fountain drink cup with a purchase of an entree during the month of September. This includes a burrito, bowl, tacos, and/or a salad. 
Valid IDs include those with documentation from high school, community college, or university identifying them as a student. 
The limit is one free drink per customer and it must be redeemed simultaneously in a single visit. The promotion is based on availability.
Offer excludes drinks such as beer, margaritas, etc. and is valid in store only.
