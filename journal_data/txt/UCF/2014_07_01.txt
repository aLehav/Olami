Red Hot & Boom on July 3, 2014
Held at Cranes Roost Park in Altamonte Springs, Red Hot & Boom is one of Orlando’s most popular annual pre-July 4 celebrations. The event is free to the public and will take place from 10 a.m. – 11p.m. with the fireworks starting at 9:30 p.m. 
This year’s artist lineup includes Alex & Sierra, American Authors, A Great Big World, Ingrid Michaelson, Echosmith and Alex Angelo.
 City of Orlando Fireworks at the Fountain on July 4
The City of Orlando will hold its annual celebration at Lake Eola Park from 4 p.m. to 10 p.m. The event is free to the public with food and beverage available for purchase. 
The fireworks for the event will begin at 9 p.m. The event will feature live music on the K92.3 stage and the Lake Eola Amphitheatre. The evening includes music from the Orlando Concert Band, who will perform at 7:30 p.m. At 8:30 p.m. guests in the park will hear a patriotic performance from the Orlando Philharmonic Orchestra, who will play at the event for the first time. 
The fireworks will be televised live on WFTV Channel 9.
Disney’s Celebrate America – A Fourth of July Concert in the Sky
It wouldn’t be Central Florida fireworks without Disney having a show of their own. Disney is having two firework celebrations on July 3 and 4 with both shows starting at 9 p.m. One-day park tickets start at $99.
Ron Jon Surf Shop & City of Cocoa Beach Annual Fireworks Display on July 5
Ron Jon Surf Shop and the City of Cocoa Beach is having their annual fireworks spectacular on July 5 at Sidney Fischer Park. The fireworks start at 8 p.m. and is free to the public.
