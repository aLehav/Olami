The Sigma Chi fraternity at UCF is still on emergency interim suspension following their hearing on Friday morning. KnightNews.com was able to catch up with Sigma Chi President, David Barley, following the hearing to learn more about how the deliberations went and what he thinks of the Greek Life suspension at UCF.

The public was denied access to the hearing, but Barley confirmed that Sigma Chi will remain on the emergency suspension until another hearing is held, which he estimated to be 2-3 weeks away.
