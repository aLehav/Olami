UCF is coming to you!
New head football coach Scott Frost, Athletic Director Danny White and other Knights kicked off their Charge on tour tonight at the Cheyenne Saloon in Downtown Orlando.
This stop is one of many that the Knights will be making across the state of Florida where Knights fans can get a chance to meet Scott Frost as he prepares for his inaugural season as head coach.
At each stop of the Charge On tour, activities will be held from 6-8 p.m. with a social hour from 6-7 p.m. and an autograph session from 6-6:30 p.m. on a first-come, first-served basis.  Below is a schedule of each stop of the tour where fans can meet the UCF athletic department.
May 17 – Downtown Orlando.
Location: Cheyenne Saloon (126 W. Church St. Orlando, FL 32801)
May 18 – Cocoa Beach
Location: Coconuts on the Beach (2 Minutemen Causeway, Cocoa Beach, FL 32931)
May 19 – Tampa.
Location: Jackson’s Bistro (601 S. Harbour Island Blvd. Tampa, FL 33602)
May 23 – Jacksonville.
Location: EverBank Field (1 EverBank Field Drive. Jacksonville, FL 32202)
May 24 – West Palm Beach.
Location: Brother Jimmy’s (700 S. Rosemary Ave. Suite 232, West Palm Beach, FL 33401)
May 25 – Ft. Lauderdale.
Location: America’s Backyard (100 SW 3rd Ave. Downtown Ft. Lauderdale, FL 33312)
More details of the tour can be found on the official website.
