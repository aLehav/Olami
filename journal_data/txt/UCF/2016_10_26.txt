The University of Central Florida’s Zeta Tau Alpha will be hosting their annual Zeta Lip Sync dance competition Thursday night at the CFE Arena on campus. 
Doors will open at 6:00 p.m. and the competition will start at 7:00 p.m.

Tickets can be purchased for $7 at the door or tonight at Cold Stone’s Creamery on University Blvd. from 6:00-9:00 p.m. during their partial proceeds event.
Money raised from participants and ticket sales will benefit Zeta Tau Alpha’s philanthropy, Breast Cancer Education and Awareness.
Knight News will be at the event on Thursday to film the dances for everyone to watch after the competition is over.
