KnightNews.com has obtained a copy of the incident report from UCF, regarding the recent death of Ann Hefferin.
You can view the report by clicking below. 
UCF Incident Report (1)
UCF spokesman Grant Heston told Knightnews.com that, “The University of Central Florida Police Department continues to investigate the death of freshman Ann Marie Hefferin.” 
He also goes on to explain why there are redactions in the report. “Because this is an active investigation, witness information has been redacted, as well as information that is still being investigated,” he said.
