Former CEO of Godfather’s Pizza and former GOP presidential candidate Herman Cain will be at UCF on Wednesday as part of his Campus Solutions tour.
Cain will be in the Cape Florida Ballroom located in the UCF Student Union from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. on Wednesday, September 3.
The seating for the event is open to the public, on a first-come, first-serve basis.
Cain previously visited UCF in 2012 as part of his College Truth Tour in hopes to teach students about the reality of college as it is today.
Currently, Cain is the host of “Cain 24/7” a nationally syndicated radio show that covers hot national topics such as politics and economics.
