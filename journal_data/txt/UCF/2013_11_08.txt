Hundreds of UCF students participated in the nation’s number one homecoming tradition — Spirit Splash!
UCF alumnus Dante Culpepper cheered on the Knights, and UCF football’s head coach George O’Leary encouraged students to attend Saturday’s game against Houston just before students charged into the water.
