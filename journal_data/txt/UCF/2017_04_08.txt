The Phi Delta Theta Chapter at the University of Central Florida will be holding a car wash tomorrow, April 9 from 11-3 p.m. for their brother Radley Gillis.
The car wash will be held at The Phi Delta Theta House, 3600 Khayyam Ave, Orlando, FL 32826. All proceeds will go to the Gillis family.
As Knight News previously reported, Gillis was involved in a serious motorcycle accident on Tuesday April 4. Since the accident, a GoFundMe for Gillis has raised over 75,000 dollars.
