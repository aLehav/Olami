The “Piano Man” himself, Billy Joel will be making his way back to Orlando for a concert on Jan. 27. 
The announcement was made Wednesday morning in a press conference with Orlando Mayor Buddy Dyer. 
Billy Joel is considered one of the best artists of all time with over 150 million records sold and 33 Top 40 hits. Joel brought the world many classics such as: “We Didn’t Start the Fire,” “Uptown Girl” and “Scenes From An Italian Restaurant.”
If you’re interested in attending this once in a lifetime performance, tickets go on sale Oct. 21 at 10 a.m.
Prices range from $49.50-$149.50. 
Tickets can be purchased online, the Chase Box Office at Amway Center, Ticketmaster.com, or by phone at 800-745-3000.
