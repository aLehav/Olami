The strongest teenager in the world is a UCF student.
Nate McCoole, a 19-year old political science major at UCF, set an all-time teen record at his recent meet, lifting a total of 2073 pounds in bench press, squat and deadlift. Split apart, McCoole benched 502, squatted 772 and deadlifted 800.
