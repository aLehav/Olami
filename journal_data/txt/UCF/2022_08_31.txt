Homecoming is right around the corner and UCF has set designated times for a limited number of tickets to experience Concert Knight and Knight of Comedy featuring Marlon Wayans. 
Wayans, an American comedian, writer and producer who starred in hit films such as White Chicks, Scary Movie 1 and 2, and the 90’s WB Sitcom, The Wayan Bros. Is set to headline this year’s Knight of Comedy for UCF’s 2022 Homecoming Celebration. 
UCF Homecoming took to their social media accounts to release the set dates that students can pick up their free tickets to this year’s Concert Knight featuring T-Pain and Knight of Comedy featuring Marlon Wayans.

Tickets for both events will be very limited with only a certain number of tickets available for pickup on each day.  
TICKETS WILL BE ON A FIRST COME, FIRST SERVED BASIS. 
Students will have to bring their Student UCF IDs in order to pick up a complimentary ticket. 
Concert Knight will be held on Oct. 27 at 8 p.m. with doors opening at 7 p.m.
 Knight of Comedy will be held on Oct. 26 at 8 p.m. with doors opening at 7 p.m. as well. 
General admission will be allowed at both events with tickets being set at $25 dollars each. You can buy your tickets now for both of UCF’s 2022 Homecoming shows at Ticketmaster.
