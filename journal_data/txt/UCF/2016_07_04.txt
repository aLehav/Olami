A 19-year-old woman was robbed by a masked man at gunpoint when walking to her apartment near the University of Central Florida, according to News 6.
It happened early Monday at around 4:25 a.m. on the 3200 block of N. Alafaya Trail, News 6 reported.
Orange County deputies told News 6 that the man approached her with a handgun and demanded her “property.”
Deputies also told News 6 that the man was about 20 years old and drove off after the robbery.
The woman suffered minor injuries, News 6 reported.
No more details have been released.
