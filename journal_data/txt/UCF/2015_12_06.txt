Hundreds gathered in attendance for Hillary Clinton as she spoke at a campaign rally in the Orlando Magic Recreation Center on Wednesday, December 2nd.
This was the former First Lady, Senator and Secretary of State’s fourth trip to Florida since the campaign began. Clinton touched on a number of issues that have been widely discussed in the elections thus far, such as college debt, immigration, and issues regarding women’s rights, such as Planned Parenthood.

Hillary Clinton is currently the front-runner in the Primary for the Democratic Party, and will continue campaigning across the nation in an effort to maintain the lead in the polls.
