Tonight, Orlando will come together once again to honor those lost in the Pulse tragedy.
A vigil will be held at Lake Eola Park at 6pm. According to a press release from the City of Orlando, among those in attendance will be Governor Rick Scott, Mayor Buddy Dyer, and Police Chief John Mina.
The release stated parking would be available in select garages for a reduced rate of $4:
Lynx will also be providing free transportation to and from the vigil at select locations. To see the list of locations, check www.golynx.com
Those who wish to help victims  of the Pulse shooting are encouraged to donate via www.oneorlando.org
