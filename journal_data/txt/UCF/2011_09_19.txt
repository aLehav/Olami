KnightNews.com has learned that Lupe Fiasco and Panic at the Disco will headline UCF homecoming 2011. 
The concert will happen on Oct. 22 2011. 
KnightNews.com has learned the concert tickets are free to UCF students with a valid UCF student ID. The tickets can be picked up at the following times and places: 
Oct. 3-7 | 9 a.m.- 7 p.m. at the Office of Student Involvement on the second floor of the Student Union
Oct. 10-21 | 9 a.m. – 7 p.m. at the SGA Ticket Center on the first floor of the Student Union
If you’re not a UCF student, or if Homecoming runs out of tickets before you get one, you can still attend the concert. General tickets go on sale with Ticketmaster on Saturday, October 1. 
Check back to KnightNews.com for more information on UCF Homecoming 2011 as we get it.
