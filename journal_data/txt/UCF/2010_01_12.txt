KnightNews.com has obtained the following list of Greek Week pairings for 2010. Greek week is a week of events similar to homecoming, that UCF fraternity and sorority members put on for themselves. It includes the signature “Greek Sing” event, similar to homecoming Skit Knight and Kappa Delta Shakedown and Zeta Lipsync.
