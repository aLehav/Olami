KnightNews.com has just obtained the full list of fraternity and sorority grade point averages and sizes for Spring 2013.
Phi Delta Theta remains the largest fraternity on campus with 127 members, followed by Theta Chi (120) and Zeta Beta Tau (105). Alpha Delta Pi also remains the largest sorority at UCF with 170 members, followed by Alpha Xi Delta/Alpha Epsilon Phi (163) and Pi Beta Phi (161).

Here are the top three highest Greek GPA organizations:
Fraternity
1. Alpha Phi Alpha – 3.201
2. Delta Lambda Phi – 3.180
3. Beta Theta Pi – 3.158
Sorority
1. Alpha Epsilon Phi – 3.354
2. Kappa Delta – 3.345
3. Alpha Delta Pi – 3.325
The overall GPA for sorority women was 3.241 and the overall GPA for fraternity men was 2.992. There are a total of 3,121 Greeks at UCF.
