Ever wish you could see and hear the person you’re chatting with on Facebook? Well, now you can. 
Facebook and Skype have teamed up to provide a new service called video calling. You can activate the service on your Facebook account at Facebook.com/videocalling. 
The launch of this new Facebook services comes around the same time Google has begun testing it’s new social network platform called Google +.
