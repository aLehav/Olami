As the Spring Break season heats up, Universal Studios Orlando is continuing to capitalize on Orlando’s status as the No. 1 Spring Break destination with its Mardi Gras events and concerts.
Universal Mardi Gras Brings Concerts During Spring Break
KnightNews.com is looking ahead to some of the concerts coming up at Universal Orlando. 
Foreigner will perform its concert tomorrow, March 12, 2011 at 8:30 p.m. The music group, according to Universal Studios Orlando, is hailed universally “as one of the most popular rock acts in the world” with “scores of smash hits, multi-platinum albums, and sold out concert tours in their time.” The group has sold more than 70 million albums across the globe, with hits such as “Cold As Ice”, “Hot Blooded”, “Waiting For a Girl Like You”, “I Want to Know What Love Is”, “Urgent” and “Double Vision.”
Next week, on Friday March 18, 2011 at 8:30 p.m., Neon Trees will take the stage at Universal Studios Orlando. The popular group, with hits such as “Animal” has made its round performing on Jimmy Kimmel Live, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Lopez Tonight, Live! with Regis & Kelly, TBS’ Conan and Late Night with Jimmy Fallon, and according to Universal Studios, they have skyrocketed in popularity with their debut album released on March 16, 2010.
