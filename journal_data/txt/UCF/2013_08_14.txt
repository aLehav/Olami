Confused about the rules of football? In case you were, UCF Football’s head coach George O’leary has a new video to help.

The UCF Knights and the Penn State Nittany Lions will  travel to Ireland to open the 2014 football season at Croke Park in Dublin. The video is most likely directed towards fans in Ireland or other countries where American football in not as prominent a sport. 
“If you are a little confused about the rules of American Football, here’s UCF Head Coach George O’Leary to help you out,” the Facebook video’s descriptions says.
The game is scheduled for 8:30 a.m. ET Saturday, Aug, 30, 2014, and will air on ESPN2. For more details on the game, click here.
