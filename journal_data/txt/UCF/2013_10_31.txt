Police are searching for an armed robber who demanded cash from a local ice cream eatery.
According to WKMG Local 6, Orange County deputies are searching for the armed culprit who robbed a Twistee Treat store Thursday morning–all while dressed in a Halloween costume.
Local 6 reports the man walked into the Twistee Treat on Alafaya Trail just after 11 a.m. wearing a costume and pointed a gun at an employee, demanding money before leaving through the back entrance.
It’s not clear what costume the robber was wearing.
