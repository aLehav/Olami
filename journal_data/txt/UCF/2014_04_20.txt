A French foreign-exchange student was killed at an intersection near UCF on Sunday morning.
Geoffrey Pimpernelle, 22, was attempting to cross University Boulevard and was struck by a 2008 Honda Civic, according to Florida Highway Patrol. Pimpernelle died at the scene of the accident.
The driver tested negatively for alcohol. Florida Highway Patrol said that Pimpernelle was crossing the street improperly and failed to yield the right of way.
The investigation is still ongoing.
