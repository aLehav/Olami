UCF will face off against AAC foe Tulsa on Friday at 8 p.m. and the weekday tailgate will mean a few changes to the operating schedule of the university. 
UCF will hold classes until 3 p.m. and faculty members that have classes that run through 3 p.m. may conduct that portion of the class up until 3 p.m.
The open-container waiver that allows alcohol to be consumed on campus for fans 21 and up will be in effect starting at 4 p.m. up until kickoff.
Most campus parking lots will open up to fans at 4 p.m. The E lots and Garage F will open up at 2 p.m. for fans with Golden Knights Club or UCF Athletics Association parking passes. 
With these lot opening times, standard university parking policies will be in effect until 3 p.m. Students staying on campus after 3 p.m. may park in garages A, B and I, the Libra Garage or lots B5, B6, B9, B12, B13 and H4. Vehicles without game day parking permits are subject to towing beginning at 3 p.m. in lots B4, B7, C1-C3, D1-D2, all E lots; in Garages C, D, F and H. 
UCF will transition back to regular operations starting Saturday at 6 a.m.
