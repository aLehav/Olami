They’re off!
The UCF Knights Football team departed from the school Tuesday to begin their journey to Ireland. The team will be in Ireland all week and will face Penn State at the Croke Park Classic in Dublin on Aug. 30. Kick-off is at 8:30 a.m. (EST).
KnightNews.com’s crew just arrived in Dublin! Watch a message from the team who will be bringing you coverage throughout the week.

Not able to make it out to Ireland for the game and looking for a place to watch?
Look no further. We’ve compiled a list of some of the watch parties happening around Orlando!

4000 Central Florida Blvd.
Orland, FL
