UCF’s Zeta Tau Alpha hosted their annual philanthropy Thursday night at CFE Arena. 
Over $52,000 dollars was raised by the chapter and the organizations who participated. 
The money raised will go towards benefitting Zeta Tau Alpha’s philanthropy, Breast Cancer Education and Awareness.
Winners for fraternities included: Sigma Chi (1st), Zeta Beta Tau (2nd), and Kappa Sigma (3rd).
Winners for sororities included: Alpha Delta Pi (1st), Delta Delta Delta (2nd), and Pi Beta Phi (3rd).
