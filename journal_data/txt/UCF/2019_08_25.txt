The Student Government Association is giving 100 students free tickets and a round-trip bus ride to Boca Raton to support our gridiron Knights at the UCF at FAU football game on Sep. 7.
First come, first serve tickets will become available on Monday at 9 a.m. at the Live Oak Ballroom near Garage B for students with a UCF ID in hand. Students can begin lining up as soon as 8: 30 a.m.
Only students present with an ID are eligible to receive the voucher and students who do receive the voucher my return back to UCF.
