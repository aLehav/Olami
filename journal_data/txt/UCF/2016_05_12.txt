The world’s best veteran and active-duty participants competed in  final track and field competitions of the Invictus Games in Orlando on Tuesday, May 10 2016. The competition featured 40 different events with the opportunity to earn 288 medals. The multiple heats were based on injury level and included the 100-meter dash, 200-meter, 400-meter, 1500-meter races and mixed 4×100 relays, as well as both, sitting and standing discus and shot put.
