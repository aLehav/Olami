The Gamma Phi Beta sorority at UCF will be holding a memorial service for sister Savannah Worrall, who passed away on Jan. 5.
The memorial will take place on Friday, Jan. 17 at 6 p.m. at the Reflection Pond on UCF campus. The campus community as well as other Greek organizations are welcomed to attend the memorial.
Savannah was a graduate of South Dade High School in Homestead, Florida. Savannah is survived by her parents and younger sister.
“Our university family extends our deepest sympathies to Savannah’s friends, sorority sisters and family members,” UCF Spokesman Chad Binette said.
