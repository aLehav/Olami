According to WESH, Orange County Fire Rescue responded to a call late this morning reporting that someone had fallen inside the Physical Sciences building. Authorities said the person fell from a ladder on the second floor.
A ladder truck and rescue helicopter are at the scene, as well as a WESH News Channel 2 helicopter.
The patient has suffered a severe skull fracture and injuries to their upper body, and was airlifted out.
