The UCF Knights (3-1) dropped their first game of the season to the #12 South Carolina Gamecocks (3-1) on Saturday.
The Knights, however, managed to stay in the game through the end leading to a final score of 28-25.
“There’s no quit in us, I knew that they would never quit,” Coach O’Leary said during the post game conference.
