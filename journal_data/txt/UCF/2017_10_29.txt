ORLANDO, Fla. – The Knights’ climb up the polls continues in the wake of a record-setting 73-point victory over Austin Peay, rising to No. 15 in the AP Poll and No. 14 in the USA Today Coaches’ Poll.
USF sunk out of the rankings after losing to Houston, and Memphis – who leads the AAC West Division – falls in line at No. 22.
The battle of the undefeated has lost its moment in the sun, but if both teams can win out until Black Friday, the game will still decide who plays in the conference championship.
