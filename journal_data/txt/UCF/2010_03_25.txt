Greek Week continued this Wednesday night with a block party. The party was well attended and went from 8:30 pm to 12 am. It took place on Greek Row thanks to the police who closed off the road. Not only was the event fun, but it also helped charity with a canned food drive.

Many organizations also supported the event by donating food. Some of the activities included were an oxygen bar, massage chairs,a live band, an inflatable slide, and a digital photo booth.The event was a success according to the people who attended. Also, it was notable that all four Greek Councils attended, which was the goal of the management of the event.
