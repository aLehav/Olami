Chipotle is making it up to its customers whose lunch plans may have been ruined today by their nationwide meeting that in result closed all Chipotle restaurants until 3 p.m.

Chipotle lovers can earn a free burrito by texting “raincheck” to 888-222. 
Consumers have until 6 p.m. Eastern Standard Time to send the text message, or until supplies last. 
Once consumers text the code, they will receive a message that will prompt them to another website where they will receive a message that says a free burrito coupon will be sent to them in the next couple days.
The nearest UCF locations are on University Blvd. and in Waterford Lakes.
