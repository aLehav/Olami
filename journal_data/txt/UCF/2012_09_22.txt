Around 2,000 students filed into the UCF Venue on Saturday to see GOP Vice Presidential Candidate Paul Ryan speak. 
The speech was aimed at Central Florida residents, especially college students, touching on topics like space, jobs and the economy. He also outlined the need to fix the national budget and cut government spending.
