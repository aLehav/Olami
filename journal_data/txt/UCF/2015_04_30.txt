Former UCF wide receiver Breshad Perriman has been drafted No. 26 overall by the Baltimore Ravens in the 2015 NFL Draft. 
With Perriman being drafted No. 26 overall, that makes it so a Knight has been drafted in the first round of the NFL Draft in back-to-back years. 
Perriman grabbed the nation’s attention at his pro day this past March, clocking an unofficial 4.22 in front of all 32 NFL teams. 
Perriman, a 6’2”, 212-pound wide receiver, finished his college career at UCF with 115 receptions for 2,243 yards and 16 touchdowns.
