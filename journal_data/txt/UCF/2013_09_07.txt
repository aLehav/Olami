Looks like the rivalry between UCF-USF has heated up a little bit more.
UCFrejects.com has been making its way across popular UCF forums and Facebook pages. When you click the link to go to the site, it automatically redirects to the USF.edu homepage.
KnightNews.com tried to look up the owner of the domain for the site, but we couldn’t find any conclusive owner of who made the site.
Recently, this photo was on the UCF reddit page, it looks like USF is selling these anti-UCF shirts in their merchandise stores. However, USF’s season has gotten off to a slow start, they dropped their first game 53-21 against Southland conference team McNeese State.
The rivalry will come to a head when USF travels to Brighthouse Networks Stadium on Nov. 29 to take on the Knights in a Black Friday game.
