Paramore will be headlining at UCF this November, the UCF Arena announced Monday.
The pop-rock group’s “The Self-Titled Tour” will take stage at the CFE Arena (formerly known as the UCf Arena) on Tuesday, November 5 at 7:30 p.m.
Special guests Metric and Hellogoodbye will be opening the night.
Ticket sales begin Friday at 10 a.m. Tickets can be purchased at the UCF Arena Box Office, charge by phone at 800-745-3000, online at www.ticketmaster.com.



UCF parking decals are on sale as of Monday, July 22nd.
As always, students will need a permit to park on campus or else they will risk being issued a parking citation.
Decals will remain the same price as last year, a full year decal will cost $95.88 and a hang tag will cost $143.82. Current parking permits will expire on August 31, 2014.
If one receives a citation, they can either be paid online at the UCF parking website or can be appealed by filling out an appeal citation within 10 business days of receiving the ticket.
