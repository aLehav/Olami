The 2016 Invictus Games in Orlando came to an end on Thursday, May 12, 2016. The closing ceremony at Disney’s ESPN Wide World of Sports featured appearances by Prince Harry, the event’s founder, Dr. Jill Biden, John Cena, and performances by Philip Phillips, Rachel Platten, Rascal Flatts, and Flo Rida. The Invictus Games’ next stop will be in Toronto, Canada in 2017.
