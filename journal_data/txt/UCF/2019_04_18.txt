The State of Florida publishes most university salaries online as part of its open government transparency initiatives.
Knight News sorted the UCF salaries by last name. 
Because some employees are funded from multiple sources of state funds, you may see two names listed twice in a row. Add both lines together to get the full salary.
The salaries are listed alphabetically, and broken down into several sections.
