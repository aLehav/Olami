The Orange County Sheriff’s Office is asking for help from KnightNews.com readers to catch a burglary suspect striking businesses near UCF. 
In a tweet to KnightNews.com, OCSO called the man caught on video a “serial burglar” who is hitting businesses and cars on the east side of Orange County. One such area hit is by University Blvd. and Goldenrod, on Metric Drive. 
OCSO says their suspect shown in the video is 20-25 years old, approximately 5’8″ with a thin build, short hair, goatee and mustache.
