As the year nears its end, the live music scene in the Tampa Bay area is starting to pick up steam again, producing a strong calendar to help you ease into November.


According to statemedia.com, there are 15 concerts just between today and Nov. 8 coming to Tampa and St. Petersburg – and that doesn’t include smaller venues such as the New World Brewery.
