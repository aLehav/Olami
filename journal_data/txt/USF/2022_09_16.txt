University Police is conducting an investigation so no other information was known at the time of publication. SPECIAL TO THE ORACLE/USF


University Police is currently investigating an on-campus death, according to UP Public Information Representative Audrey Clarke.


A body was found near the Beard Garage sometime Friday morning. The exact time of death was not known, according to UP.


UP is investigating the death as a potential suicide. It was unknown if the victim was a student.


This is a developing story.


If you or someone you know is contemplating suicide, call or text 988 to reach the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.
