USF will confer 1,500 degrees over two commencement ceremonies at 9 a.m. and 1:30 p.m. on Saturday, as part of the university’s
100th
commencement ceremonies.


More than 700 students from the College of Arts and Sciences, the College of Business and the St. Petersburg campus colleges are expected to graduate during the 9 a.m. ceremony, and more than 750 students from all other colleges and the Sarasota-Manatee campus are expected to graduate during the 1 p.m. ceremony, according to a press release.


Each ceremony is expected to last 90 minutes. Doors open 90 minutes before each ceremony and parking is free.
