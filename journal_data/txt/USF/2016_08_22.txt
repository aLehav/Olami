USF football released its new "So-Flo Remix" uniforms on Twitter Monday, which will be worn for the Bulls' debut on Sept. 3. SPECIAL TO THE ORACLE






USF Football released both a promotional video and accompanying pictures for the Bulls’ “So-Flo Remix” uniforms, which the team will wear in its season opener against Towson University on Sept. 3.


The promotional video features an elevator door sliding open to reveal senior defensive back Nate Godwin showcasing the new attire, which is dark green with neon green stripes and numbers highlighting the uniform.


The new helmets paired with the uniform are dark green and feature a row of light green to neon green stripes decorating the middle of the helmet.
