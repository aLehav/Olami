USF men’s soccer senior defender Ben Sweat was drafted
14th
overall in the Major League Soccer (MLS)
SuperDraft

Sweat started all 21 matches for the Bulls in his senior season and was named All-American Athletic Conference First-Team. Sweat played in 78 games as a Bull, a USF individual record.

Precourt

4th
