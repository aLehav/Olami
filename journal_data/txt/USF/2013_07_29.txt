An armed carjacking was reported on campus at approximately 9:20 a.m. Monday morning.


Vladimir Matushevskiy, 29, a suspect in connection with the carjacking, was arrested in Marion County on Monday afternoon according to an article published by WTSP 10 News, after reportedly threatening a woman with a gun and asking her to get out of her car.


Marion deputies said Matushevskiy might not be the only person involved in the carjacking.


No injuries were reported.
