Bio:


Bryan Friesen’s passion for skateboarding was the spark that ignited his interest in video production and editing. He has edited, taped and produced skateboarding video, including coverage of the Mountain Dew Tour and other skateboarding contests such as the Tampa Pro.

As the executive producer of a 30-minute documentary and by completing an internship at the Home Shopping Network, Bryan sharpened his video production and reporting skills. His experience in production is matched with a plethora of knowledge in Web development and management. He worked as The Oracle’s online editor from spring 2009 to spring 2010.

A senior majoring in television production, Bryan interns at Channel 32 (WMOR). He hopes to be a television producer with a focus on educational and extreme sports programming.
