The Department of Public Safety reported that a motor vehicle was stolen from the Leavey Garage Friday. The theft occurred between the hours of 7:00 a.m. and 3:30 p.m. The vehicle is described as a black Buddy moped with a damaged front left light.
DPS is investigating the incident.
