An unknown intruder stole a Georgetown employee’s wallet from the employee’s unlocked office on the second floor of Poulton Hall on Tuesday afternoon, according to an alert from the Department of Public Safety.
The Public Safety Alert said that the burglary was reported at 3:06 p.m. and occurred between 1:15 and 3 p.m. There were no signs of forced entry.
DPS described two suspects both in their late 20s to early 30s wearing white T-shirts and jeans. The first suspect is a black male of dark complexion between 5 feet 11 inches and 6 feet tall and of average build. The second suspect is a black female of dark complexion between 5 feet 4 inches and 5 feet 5 inches and of heavy build.
DPS requests that anyone with information regarding the incident contact them immediately.
