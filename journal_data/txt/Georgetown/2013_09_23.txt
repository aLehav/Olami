A locked office in the Berkley Center was burglarized over the weekend, the Department of Public Safety reported today.
The incident occurred between Friday at 5:15 p.m. and Sunday at 10:45 a.m., when an unknown intruder forced open the secured door and stole a digital camera dna  laptop computer.
There is currently no description available of the suspects. DPS requests that anyone with information regarding the incident contact them immediately.
