Four suspects robbed a victim on the 3200 block of Prospect Street Northwest on Sunday morning, the Department of Public Safety reported.
The incident occurred at approximately 3 a.m., when the victim was punched in the face and robbed of an unknown amount of money. According to DPS, the victim is unaffiliated with the university.
There is currently no description available of the suspects, but the Metropolitan Police are investigating the incident. DPS requests that anyone with information regarding the incident contact them immediately.
