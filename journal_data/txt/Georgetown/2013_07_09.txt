An unlocked room on the fourth floor of Darnall Hall was burglarized Monday, the Department of Public Safety reported.
The incident occurred between 9:00 a.m. and 10:45 p.m., when the intruder stole a wallet and a passport from a dresser drawer.
There is currently no description available of the suspects. DPS requests that anyone with information regarding the incident contact them immediately.
