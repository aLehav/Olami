A desktop computer and safe were stolen from an office in Darnall Hall Wednesday morning, according to the Department of Public Safety.
DPS reported the burglary in a public safety alert emailed to the campus community at 1:50 p.m. Wednesday. According to the alert, a staff member discovered that an administrative office suite been broken into and burglarized at 7:27 a.m.
The alert does not list any suspects who may have been involved in the incident.
