
April 5, 2012 by Sports Editor Leave a Comment 
Thursday, March 29, 2012
Simple Assault, 36th and Prospect Streets, 1:24 a.m.
A student reported that while seated in the rear seat of a taxi cab the driver turned around and began rubbing her left thigh.
Theft, ICC, 1:52 p.m.
A staff member reported the theft of their wallet containing $131.00 and a credit card.
Friday, March 30, 2012
Alcohol Violation, McDonough Gymnasium, 10:28 p.m.
A Department of Public Safety officer reported finding a student in possession of an alcoholic beverage. The case was referred to the Office of Student Conduct.
Assault, McDonough Gymnasium, 11:30 p.m.
A DPS officer reported that two officers were physically assaulted by a student. Case closed with one arrest.
Saturday, March 31, 2012
Possession of Marijuana, Observatory, 2:36 a.m.
A DPS officer reported finding a group of subjects in possession of a green weed substance.
Sunday, April 1, 2012
Destruction of Property, Red Square, 1:36 a.m.
A DPS officer reported seeing  a student flip over a bench causing damage. The case was referred to the Office of Student Conduct
Tuesday, April 3, 2012
Burglary, Kennedy Hall, 8:30 p.m.
A student reported that an unknown suspect gained entry into his or her room by unknown means and took several items.
The blotter is compiled weekly by the Department of Public Safety.
