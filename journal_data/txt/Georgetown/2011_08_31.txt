Two students were threatened with assault Monday night while walking near Henle Village, according to the Department of Public Safety.
According to the Public Safety Alert emailed to the campus community Wednesday morning, the students were followed by seven unknown white males while walking on campus. One of the males threatened the victims with assault, and they began to quickly walk toward the front gates.
The PSA identifies the suspects as being in their late teens to early twenties. Six of the suspects were wearing backwards baseball caps. One suspect is described as having blonde hair and light colored eyes with a lean build. According to the PSA, he is approximately 6 feet 2 inches tall, weighs 175 pounds and was wearing khaki pants and a polo shirt.
Representatives of DPS could not be reached for comment at press time for this article.
