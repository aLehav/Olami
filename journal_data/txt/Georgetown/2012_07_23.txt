An unidentified male exposed himself to students near Village A Saturday morning, the Department of Public Safety reported.
According to a public safety alert email sent to the campus community Monday afternoon, Georgetown students were approached by the male at 9:15 am on Saturday while walking along Prospect Street near Village A. The suspect exposed himself to the students, who walked away from the scene
The suspect, who was described as a 6-foot, muscular Hispanic male, made no contact with the students.
This is the first sex offense reported this summer. It was referred to the Metropolitan Police Department, which is conducting an investigation.
