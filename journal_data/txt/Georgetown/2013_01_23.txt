An unknown male exposed himself to a student in the alley entrance of the 3600 block of P Street NW this morning, according to a Department of Public Safety alert.
The incident occurred around 8:45 a.m. and the victim was not hurt.
The suspect is described as a Hispanic male of medium build having curly black hair and approximately 5’7” tall. He was reportedly wearing a red v-neck sweater with white trim and blue jeans.
DPS is investigating the incident.
